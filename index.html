THE ARKHAM GAMES

The best Batman series in the gaming industry that is respected many DC fans and non fans.

The series lasted in 2009 to 2015 until 2024 with Suicide Squad: Kill The Justice League as a follow-up from Arkham Knight but unfortunately is not appreciated by fans as it didn't get the expectations from people who enjoyed the Arkham Games.

Arkham Asylum

Welcome to the Madhouse! Descend into the core of the criminally insane as The Joker launches his most diabolical plot ever. Explore the depths of this never-before-seen island as you experience the darkest journey of Batman's life. Written by Paul Dini and featuring the voice talents of Mark Hamill and Kevin Conroy.

Arkham City

Batman: Arkham City builds upon the intense, atmospheric foundation of Batman: Arkham Asylum, sending players soaring into Arkham City - five times larger than the game world in Batman: Arkham Asylum. This sequel introduces a brand-new story that draws together a new, all-star cast of classic characters and murderous villains from the Batman universe, as well as a vast range of new and enhanced gameplay features to deliver the ultimate experience as the Dark Knight.

Arkham Knight

In the explosive finale to the Arkham series, Batman faces the ultimate threat against the city he is sworn to protect. The Scarecrow returns to unite an impressive roster of super villains, including Penguin, Two-Face and Harley Quinn, to destroy The Dark Knight forever.
